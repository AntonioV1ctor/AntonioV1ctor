## Who I am

I'm a **backend developer** from Brazil with **1 year and 5 months** of hands-on experience, and only 18 years old. I got into tech early and kept going — building real products, learning from real problems, and shipping real code. My stack is centered around Python and Ruby on Rails on the backend, with Docker and Linux as daily tools. I write systems that actually work, not just tutorials that look good.
